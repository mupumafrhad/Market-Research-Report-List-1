<p><strong>The Sealless Magnetic Drive Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Shaft</li><li>Rotating Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sealless magnetic drive pumps are categorized into two main types based on their shaft configurations: stationary shaft and rotating shaft. Stationary shaft designs feature a fixed shaft with the pump impeller connected to a rotating magnetic coupling, allowing for efficient fluid transfer without traditional seals. In contrast, rotating shaft systems use a rotating shaft linked to both the drive and impeller, providing greater flexibility in design and operation. Both types eliminate leakage risks, enhancing safety and reliability in various industrial applications.</p></p>
