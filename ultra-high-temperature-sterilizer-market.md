<p><strong>The Ultra High Temperature Sterilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tubular Sterilizer</li><li>Craped Surface Sterilizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra High Temperature Sterilizer market includes two main types: Tubular Sterilizers and Creeped Surface Sterilizers. Tubular Sterilizers utilize a series of tubes where products pass through hot steam, achieving rapid heating and cooling, making them efficient for continuous processing. Creeped Surface Sterilizers, on the other hand, involve a heat-exchange surface where fluids are sterilized by direct contact with heated surfaces, optimizing thermal efficiency. Both types are crucial for ensuring product safety, particularly in the food and beverage sectors.</p></p>
