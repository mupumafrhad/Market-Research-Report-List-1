<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Wall Mounted Power Supplies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Output</li><li>Dual Tracking Output</li><li>Dual Isolated Output</li><li>Triple Isolated Output</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wall mounted power supplies are categorized based on their output configurations. Single output units provide a single voltage, ideal for straightforward applications. Dual tracking outputs offer two voltages that adjust simultaneously, suitable for devices needing different power levels. Dual isolated outputs ensure two separate voltages without cross-interference, enhancing safety. Triple isolated outputs provide three isolated voltages for complex systems. Other types include programmable and modular supplies, allowing flexibility and adaptation to varied power requirements for specialized applications.</p></p>
