<p><p>Projection mapping equipment primarily utilizes two types of display technologies: DLP (Digital Light Processing) and LCD (Liquid Crystal Display). DLP offers high brightness and contrast, making it suitable for large-scale projections, often used in immersive experiences. Both types cater to various applications, including entertainment, advertising, and art installations, each bringing unique strengths to projection mapping projects.</p></p>
