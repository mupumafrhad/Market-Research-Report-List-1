<p><p>The Radar Front End market primarily consists of two key frequency bands: S-Band and X-Band. S-Band, ranging from 2 to 4 GHz, is commonly used in weather radar, air traffic control, and naval applications due to its ability to penetrate rain and fog. Conversely, X-Band, operating between 8 to 12 GHz, is favored for high-resolution imaging in military radar, maritime navigation, and traffic management. Each band serves distinct operational needs, influencing equipment design and technology development in the radar sector.</p></p>
