<p><strong>The Alternating Current Dynamo Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Synchronous Machine</li><li>Asynchronous Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Alternating Current Dynamo market encompasses two primary types: synchronous machines and asynchronous motors. Synchronous machines operate at a constant speed, synchronizing with the supply frequency, making them ideal for applications requiring stable performance. In contrast, asynchronous motors, commonly known as induction motors, rely on the slip between the rotating magnetic field and rotor speed, allowing for greater versatility and efficiency in various applications. Both types play crucial roles in industrial, commercial, and residential sectors, driving advancements in energy management and automation.</p></p>
