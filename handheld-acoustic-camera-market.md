<p><p>The handheld acoustic camera market features two primary types: 2D microphone arrays and 3D microphone arrays. </p><p>2D microphone arrays consist of multiple microphones arranged in a flat configuration, enabling effective sound source localization and visualization in two dimensions. </p><p>In contrast, 3D microphone arrays incorporate a spherical or volumetric arrangement of microphones, allowing for a more immersive and comprehensive analysis of sound in three-dimensional space. </p><p>Both types cater to various applications, including surveillance, industrial testing, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
