<p><p>The generator belt market comprises two primary types: rubber belts and polyurethane (PU) belts. Rubber belts are known for their durability and resistance to wear and tear, making them ideal for applications requiring high strength and flexibility. PU belts, on the other hand, offer superior abrasion resistance, lightweight construction, and lower friction, which is beneficial for applications needing heightened precision and reduced energy consumption. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, catering to various generator configurations and operational environments.</p></p>
