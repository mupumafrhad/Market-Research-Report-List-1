<p><p>The Rectangular Water Bath market comprises primarily two types: Single Wall and Double Wall systems. Single Wall water baths are simpler, with a single layer of material, suitable for basic heating and experimentation needs. In contrast, Double Wall water baths feature an insulated design with an inner and outer layer, providing enhanced temperature stability and efficiency, making them ideal for precise applications. The choice between the two types depends on the required temperature control and specific laboratory needs.</p></p>
