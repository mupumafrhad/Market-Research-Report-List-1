<p><p>The live streaming camera market is primarily categorized into two types: wireless and wired. Wireless cameras utilize Wi-Fi or cellular connections, providing flexibility and ease of installation, ideal for dynamic settings. They are often battery-powered and allow remote access, enhancing mobility. In contrast, wired cameras rely on physical connections, such as Ethernet cables, offering stable and high-quality video streaming with minimal latency. These are typically preferred in fixed setups, such as studios or security environments, where consistent performance is essential.</p></p>
