<p><p>The potable water filters market encompasses various types, each with distinct mechanisms. Reverse Osmosis (RO) filters use a semipermeable membrane to remove dissolved solids and contaminants, providing highly purified water. UV filters utilize ultraviolet light to disinfect water by neutralizing bacteria and viruses without chemicals. Activated Carbon filters absorb impurities and chlorine, enhancing taste and odor. Together, these technologies cater to diverse consumer needs, ensuring access to safe drinking water while addressing specific water quality concerns.</p></p>
