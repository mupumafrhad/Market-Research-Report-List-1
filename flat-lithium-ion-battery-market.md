<p><p>The flat lithium-ion battery market primarily includes two types: liquified lithium-ion batteries and polymer lithium-ion batteries. Liquified lithium-ion batteries utilize liquid electrolytes, offering high energy density and efficient heat dissipation, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Conversely, polymer lithium-ion batteries use solid or gel polymer electrolytes, allowing for flexible designs and lightweight characteristics, ideal for portable devices. Both technologies cater to the growing demand for compact, high-capacity energy storage solutions in various consumer electronics and electric vehicles.</p></p>
