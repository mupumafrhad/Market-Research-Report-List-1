<p><p>Solar air heating systems can be categorized into active and passive systems. Active solar heating utilizes mechanical devices like fans and pumps to circulate air or fluids, enhancing heat transfer and providing more controlled energy distribution. In contrast, passive solar heating relies on building design and materials to naturally collect and distribute solar energy, minimizing mechanical intervention. Both types aim to harness sunlight for heating spaces, though they differ in complexity, efficiency, and reliance on technology.</p></p>
