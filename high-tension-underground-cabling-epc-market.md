PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is cost-effective and widely used for its good insulation properties. XLPE (Cross-Linked Polyethylene) offers superior thermal resistance and electrical performance, making it ideal for high-voltage applications. Rubber cables provide exceptional flexibility and resistance to environmental factors, suitable for challenging terrains. Each type has its specific advantages, catering to diverse project requirements in the underground cabling sector.</p></p>
