<p><p>The sterilization instruments market comprises several key types. Hydrogen peroxide sterilizers utilize vaporized hydrogen peroxide to eliminate microorganisms, making them ideal for heat-sensitive equipment. Moist heat or steam sterilization equipment employs high-pressure steam to achieve effective sterilization, commonly used in hospitals. Ethylene oxide sterilization involves exposing items to ethylene oxide gas, suitable for items sensitive to moisture and heat. Other methods include radiation and chemical sterilization technologies, catering to diverse applications across medical, pharmaceutical, and laboratory sectors.</p></p>
