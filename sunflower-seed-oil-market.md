<p><ul><li>Linoleic Sunflower Seed Oil</li><li>Mid-Oleic Sunflower Seed Oil</li><li>High-Oleic Sunflower Seed Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sunflower seed oil market comprises three main types: linoleic, mid-oleic, and high-oleic sunflower seed oils. Linoleic sunflower seed oil is rich in polyunsaturated fats, ideal for cooking and salad dressings. Mid-oleic sunflower seed oil has a balanced fat profile, making it suitable for frying and baking. High-oleic sunflower seed oil contains a higher monounsaturated fat content, offering better stability and a longer shelf life, often favored in industrial applications and health-conscious food products.</p></p>
