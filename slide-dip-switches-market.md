<p><ul><li>Surface-Mounting</li><li>Throgh-hole Mounting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Slide DIP switches are categorized into two main types based on their mounting styles: surface-mounting and through-hole mounting. Surface-mounting slide DIP switches are compact and designed for automated PCB assembly, making them ideal for space-sensitive applications. In contrast, through-hole mounting switches are inserted into pre-drilled holes on the circuit board, offering enhanced sturdiness and ease of manual installation. Both types serve diverse applications in consumer electronics, industrial controls, and communication devices, depending on space and durability requirements.</p></p>
