<p><p>Neti pots are available in two primary market types: electric and manual. Electric neti pots use a powered mechanism to deliver saline solution gently through the nasal passages, ensuring consistent flow and ease of use, especially for those with limited dexterity. Manual neti pots rely on gravity, requiring users to tilt their heads for effective rinsing. While electric options offer convenience, manual pots are often preferred for their simplicity and lower cost, catering to diverse consumer preferences in nasal hygiene.</p></p>
